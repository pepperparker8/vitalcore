Step-by-step: Export from Polar Flow
01

Sync your Loop 2

Open the Polar Flow app on your phone. Your Loop 2 syncs automatically via Bluetooth when it's nearby. Allow 30–60 seconds for full sync.

Polar Flow app → Home screen → sync indicator top right
02

Find your resting HR and HRV

These are calculated from your overnight sleep session. Tap on last night's sleep entry. You'll see Resting Heart Rate and, if wearing all night, an HRV reading.

Flow app → Activity → Sleep → last night → HR details
03

Find your activity data

The main Activity screen shows Steps, Active Calories, and your daily Activity Goal percentage (0–100%). These reset at midnight each day.

Flow app → Activity → Daily summary
04

Log in VitalCore

Go to Dashboard, open the input panel, and tap the orange "Polar" tab. Enter the numbers you found. VitalCore calculates your Recovery Score, Strain estimate, and Sleep Score automatically.

VitalCore → Dashboard → Log Reading → Polar tab
